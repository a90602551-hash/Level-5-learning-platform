Should Schools Have High Academic Standards?

Many educators who experts believe are successful say that schools should have high academic standards. These standards are goals that students must achieve. When schools have high expectations, students learn more and prepare better for their future.

High standards help students develop important skills. Students who teachers challenge with hard work become stronger learners. They learn to solve problems and think carefully. Schools which educators say maintain excellence produce graduates who succeed in college and career.

Teachers use methods that researchers believe work best. These include clear instructions and regular feedback. Some people worry that high standards are too difficult. However, students can succeed when they receive proper support. In the workplace, workers who managers think can handle responsibility earn better positions. High standards prepare students for adult life.

Passage Analysis

Main Claim:

Schools should have high academic standards.

Supporting Point 1:

High standards help students develop important skills and prepare for college/career.

Supporting Point 2:

Teachers use effective methods; students succeed with proper support.

Conclusion:

High standards prepare students for adult life and workplace success.
